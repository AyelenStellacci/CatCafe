Between purring and a coffee

The Crazy Cat Café is born from a great love, the love for cats, and an unforgettable experience in a Neko Café in Osaka, Japan. In October 2015, the first and only Cat Café in Lombardy opens in Milan, aiming to recreate the intimate atmosphere of Japanese neko cafés while adapting it to the rhythms and lifestyle of Italy, where the absolute masters are the nine cats that live inside the establishment.
